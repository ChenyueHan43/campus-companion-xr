# 🎤 3-Minute Demo Speech
## Campus Companion XR — SPATIAL SHANGHAI 2026

> **Audience:** judges + general audience
> **Tone:** warm, story-driven, confident
> **Duration:** ~3 minutes (≈ 450 words at comfortable pace)
> **Language:** English primary · key phrases in Chinese
> **Cue notes in [brackets] for the presenter**

---

## ── OPENING · Hook (0:00 – 0:30) ──

[Pause. Make eye contact. Speak slowly.]

"Imagine it's 2 a.m.
You're stressed about tomorrow's exam. You feel lonely.
Your friends are asleep. The counseling office won't open for seven hours.

What do you do?"

[Pause 2 seconds.]

"For one in three college students — that's not a hypothetical.
That's Tuesday night."

---

## ── PROBLEM (0:30 – 1:00) ──

"University mental health is in crisis.

A single counselor at a typical Chinese university serves five hundred students — sometimes more.
Students avoid official channels because they fear judgment, or social stigma.
And the people who need help most? They don't need lectures.
They need a *peer*. Someone their own age to just... be there.

That's the gap we're closing."

---

## ── SOLUTION DEMO (1:00 – 2:00) ──

[Open the browser. The 3D scene is already loaded.]

"This is **Campus Companion XR** — 乐乐陪伴空间.

A fully immersive, browser-based 3D campus healing space.
No VR headset. No app download. Just open a link."

[Point to the central hall.]

"You're standing in the central hall of a warm, quiet campus room.
Five zones surround you — each powered by a different AI mode."

[Click the Chat Corner platform. Camera flies in.]

"Click *Chat Corner* — and our AI companion Lele greets you.
Lele is a peer. A fellow student. Never a therapist. Never judgmental.
She listens, she validates, she's here at 2 a.m."

[Type a quick message, show AI response.]

"The AI context switches automatically with every zone.

[Click Study Room.]
Study zone — now Lele's a patient tutor.

[Click Healing Garden.]
Healing Garden — breathing exercises, calligraphy, slow living.

[Click Game Zone.]
And here — Gomoku, Go, Chinese Chess, mindful coloring.
Games aren't just fun. They're proven stress-relief tools."

[Briefly show Tripo loading status or avatar.]

"Behind the scenes, Tripo AI generates a personalized 3D avatar — your companion's face.
The student *chooses* the gender. It's their companion."

---

## ── IMPACT (2:00 – 2:35) ──

"What we've built isn't just a demo.
It's a new mental-health paradigm for campus life.

- **Zero barrier**: any student, any device, any time
- **Full privacy**: no login, no data stored, no campus system involved
- **Peer-level AI**: warmth without clinical distance
- **Scalable**: one server, unlimited concurrent students

In China alone, there are forty million college students.
Even reaching one percent of them could change lives."

## ── CLOSE (2:35 – 3:00) ──

[Slow down. Sincere tone.]

"We built this in 24 hours because we believe technology should make people
feel *less alone*, not more distracted.

Campus Companion XR — 因为每个人都值得被陪伴。
Because everyone deserves to be heard."

## 📌 Q&A Prep Notes

| Likely Question | Suggested Answer |
|---|---|
| Safety — what if student is in crisis? | Lele has a built-in trigger: if crisis keywords appear, it gently redirects to professional help and provides a hotline number. |
| Data privacy? | Zero data stored. All conversation is in-memory, session-only. No backend. |
| Why not a mobile app? | WebXR in the browser removes the install barrier — critical for mental health, where friction kills adoption. |
| Tripo latency? | ~2–3 min for first-time generation; avatar is cached after that. Placeholder is shown immediately. |
| Can universities deploy this? | Yes — it's a static folder. Any CDN or university server can host it in under 5 minutes. |
